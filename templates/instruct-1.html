Instructions

In this experiment, you will be shown a total of four videos in which someone interacts with a robot in a Create Your Own Adventure Story. You will be shown two story paths in total, and two different character perspectives - third person and second person.
Please watch the robot closely in all four videos. You will be asked to gauge your engagement and enjoyment of the stories dependent on the perspective the robot reads them. Please watch all videos attentively and answer all questions carefully.
